Personal Betterment

CEG Betterment Project Area

Initiatives in this Category

Personal Counseling

Personal Counseling is an initiative to provide mental health support to students and staff. This is informed by the campus wide survey conducted in 2020-2024.

English Communication

English Communication is an initiative to improve the communication skills of students and staff. Alumni run many workshops for improving the English communication skills. This initiaitve willl further strengthen the efforts.

Support for Student Competitions

Students participate in many competitions, and this is an initiative to support them. The survey finds that CEG does not have adequate support for students to participate in Indian, and International academic and professional competitions.

Loaner Laptop

CEG has a strong academic reputation, but it lags behind other institutions in providing essential resources to its students. Loaner Laptop is an initiative to provide laptops to students who need them.
