The CEGAANA Scholarship Fund

We provide tax-deductible donations with end-to-end transparency to show exactly where and how your contributions are spent. We work alongside the Knowledge Foundation in Chennai (run by the CEG 1990 batch) to thoroughly vet and identify deserving students.

$2,100 Funds a Day Scholar (4 Years)
$6,800 Funds a Hostel Student (4 Years)
100% Direct Funding to Students*

Student Selection Criteria

  • First-generation college attendees
  • Hailing from low-income families
  • Demonstrated interest, commitment, and excellent high school academic records
Batch-Based Funds: Want to make an impact with your peers? Alumni can combine donations within their batch (e.g., 1984, 1990, 1993, 1996) to sponsor specific students. Batch funds have successfully raised over $30,000 to date!
