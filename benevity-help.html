How to Donate via Benevity

Thank you for supporting CEG students! Please use these steps to complete your donation:

  1. Login into your company Benevity portal using single sign on. Usually it will be <companyname>.benevity.org (e.g., google.benevity.org).
  2. Search for "CEGAANA Scholarship" or just "CEGAANA".
  3. Submit your donation.
  4. Please specify the batch in the notes section.
  5. Once you donate, please fill your information in this form for the tax receipt.
Note: If you do not find CEGAANA Scholarship in your Benevity site, please reach out to finance@cegaana.org to help setup the organization. This will help all future donors.
