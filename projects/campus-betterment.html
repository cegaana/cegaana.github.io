Campus Betterment

CEG Betterment Project Area

Initiatives in this Category

Campus Cleanliness

A clean campus promotes students' mental and physical health and makes the learning experience pleasant and fun. This effort is to empower HODs to keep their facilities clean, and washrooms adequately resourced.

Canteen Hygiene

Hygiene in the canteen is important for the health of students and staff. The number of students through the years has increased substantially, but the canteen facilities have not been upgraded. This effort is to make the canteen better, and have good hygiene.

Network Connectivity

Good network connectivity is essential for students and staff to access online resources, communicate with each other, and stay connected with the outside world. This is important for the campus and the hostels.
