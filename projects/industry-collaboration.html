Industry Collaboration

CEG Betterment Project Initiative

Project Details

Many CEG departments have excellent collaboration with the industry. However, the students of departments such as Manufacturing and ECE have indicated the need for improvements. This effort will focus on creating better collaboration with the industry for these deparments, and increase project, internships and placements.

12/5/2020

A list of 21 students have applied for the program. More details to be provided soon.

10/3/2020

A proposal to provide training to non-CS and non-IT students has been crafted. It will be shared with the alumni for support.
