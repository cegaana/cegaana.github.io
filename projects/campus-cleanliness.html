Campus Cleanliness

CEG Betterment Project Initiative

Project Details

A clean campus promotes students' mental and physical health and makes the learning experience pleasant and fun. This effort is to empower HODs to keep their facilities clean, and washrooms adequately resourced.

Effort Lead: Former Dean Natarajan

10/14/2020

Dean's Message:

Request for proposal (RFP) From experienced housekeeping agencies on Quality and Cost base selection (QCBS) basis for rendering housekeeping services in the academic zones of CEG, AC Tech, SAP and MIT campuses of Anna university has been issued by the AU Registrar.

8/25/2020

Dean's Message:

The Campus Cleaning Team in Estate Office (EO) has been unified and enhanced with additional team of staff from the Dean's office to ensure campus cleanliness. The EO was kind enough to take up complete responsibility to ensure Campus Cleanliness.
