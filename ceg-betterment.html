CEG Betterment

Make CEG Student Experience A Delight

A survey of CEG students was conducted in May 2020 to understand what alumni can do to help make CEG better. And continuously we hear from visiting alumni and students about potential new needs.

The results of the survey indicated several needs as listed below. The CEG Betterment project has been initiated by the CEG alumni. We receive donations and contribute to projects in CEG. We have funded restroom facilities, UPS for IT labs, student counseling support, funding college events, and support for student competitions.

Areas for Improvement

From the survey results, the following critical needs were identified:

  • Restroom availability and cleanliness
  • Study rooms at hostels (study areas)
  • Canteen hygiene
  • Laptop loans
  • Network connectivity in hostels and campus
  • Industry initiated projects (ECE and Manufacturing)
  • Support for participating in student competitions (India & abroad)
  • Internship
  • English communication
  • Personal counseling - Emotional Well-being
