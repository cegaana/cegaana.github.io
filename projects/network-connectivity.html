Network Connectivity

CEG Betterment Project Initiative

Project Details

The performance of network connectivity is not adequate for learning. This is the case on campus, as well as in the hostels.

12/6/2020

Office of the Dean has collected the actual wired and wireless wifi requirements of each dept and centre, and the consolidated list has been submitted to RCC Director. RCC has given the estimate and it is submitted to the budget committee on 2nd Dec 2020. It comes to further discussion tomorrow.

Estate office has identified three places to install BSNL Mobile towers in Campus. Negotiations are going on for other service providers to utlilise this service on rental / lease basis. May be decided in a week time.

10/3/2020

Approval has been provided to improve the network/wifi connectivity. More details to come.
