The Old Canteen

The number of students through the years has increased substantially, but the canteen facilities have not been upgraded. This effort is to make the canteen better, and have good hygiene.

Effort Lead: Former Dean Natarajan

March 2021

The old canteen is being demolished to begin building the new one.

12/6/2020

New temporary canteen will be ready by December 25th in Centre for excellence. Currently, the canteen is operating in AC Tech new building

10/3/2020

Great news! A new Student Amenities Centre building is in the works.

8/25/2020

Canteen tenders have been assigned to vendors and negotiations are underway

Meanwhile, a hostel is operating temporary canteen for tea, coffee, and snacks for Staff and others. Lunch is also served at the hostel at concessional price to those who need, till regular canteen begins operating.
